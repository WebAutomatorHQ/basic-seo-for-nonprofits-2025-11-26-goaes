About Basic SEO for nonprofits

Created by someone who has been in your shoes.

Basic SEO for nonprofits was developed by Frederic Sune after more than a decade of building websites and digital strategies for mission-driven organizations. Over and over, he saw the same pattern: nonprofits investing in a beautiful site, only to realize months later that almost no one was finding it through search. This program distills the repeatable steps he has used with small and mid-sized nonprofits into a clear, accessible learning path that anyone on your team can follow.

The mission behind this project is simple: make effective, ethical SEO knowledge available to nonprofits that do not have in-house digital teams or large marketing budgets. Every lesson is guided by core values of clarity, transparency, and respect for your time. The goal is not to turn you into an SEO guru—but to give you just enough understanding and structure to make search engines a reliable, sustainable channel for advancing your mission.
